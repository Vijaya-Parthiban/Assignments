Tragic Stampede at TVK Rally in Karur Claims 41 Lives

On 27 September 2025, during a political rally by TVK leader Vijay held in Veluswamypuram, Karur district, a stampede caused by massive crowd surges resulted in **41 deaths** and left many injured.

The rally had been delayed significantly, and the large, restless crowd rushed towards the campaign vehicle when the leader arrived, leading to chaos and collapse in multiple zones.

Several TVK officials, including party general secretary Bussy Anand and district secretary Mathiyazhagan, have been named in the FIR and have sought anticipatory bail.

Vijay, in a video message, expressed deep sorrow and asked authorities to restrict actions to him rather than targeting party functionaries.

The state government released videos alleging breach of police cordons and violation of safety norms by the organizers, increasing the intensity of the blame game surrounding the event.
