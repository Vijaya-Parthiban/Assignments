Task 3 - Changing Padding Values

Padding: 10px

Padding is the space between the content and the border of an element. It creates space inside the box, keeping the text from touching the edges. Here, the padding is small (10px), so the content stays close to the border.

Padding: 40px

Increasing padding pushes the content further away from the border, creating a more spacious and clean look. It doesn't affect the margin or border, only the space inside the element. This box uses 40px padding to show the difference.
