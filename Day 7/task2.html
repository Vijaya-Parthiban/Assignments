Steps to make CHICKEN SOUP

  1. Wash and cut chicken into pieces.
  2. Chop vegetables like carrots, onions, and cabbage.
  3. Heat a pot and add a little oil or butter.
  4. Saute onions, garlic, and other vegetables until soft.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  6. Pour in water or chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  8. Add salt, pepper, and green onion to taste.
  9. Remove from heat and serve hot.
