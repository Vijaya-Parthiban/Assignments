Importance of Healthy Lifestyle

Why Health Matters

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle is very important in today's world. It requires discipline,regular exercise, and enough rest. Doctors advise everyone to drink at least 2 liters of water daily . Avoiding junk food is equally essential for long-term health

Small but Effective Changes

Nutritionists recommend reducing oily snacks and replacing them with fresh fruits and vegetables. Our body also needs essential compounds like water (H2O) and minerals such as calcium ions (Ca2+). Even simple lifestyle adjustments can make a big difference.

Daily Habits for Good Health

  1. Exercise for atleast 30 minutes daily.
  2. Eat balanced meals rich in vegetables and proteins.
  3. Drink plenty of water.
  4. Avoid midnight junk food and prefer home-cooked meals.
  5. Sleep atleast 7-8 hours each night.

Foods to include in your Diet

  • Green leafy vegetables like spinach and greens
  • Fruits- apples, bananas, oranges
  • Protein-rich foods - eggs, pulses, fish
  • Low-fat dairy products such as yogurt and milk
